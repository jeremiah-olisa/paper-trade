Our Mission

At Paper Trader, we believe that successful trading begins with education and practice. Our mission is to provide a risk-free environment where traders of all levels can develop their skills, test strategies, and build confidence before entering the real markets.

We're committed to democratizing access to professional-grade trading tools and real-time market data, making advanced trading education accessible to everyone.

Our Story

Paper Trader was born from a simple observation: too many aspiring traders were losing money because they lacked proper education and practice opportunities.

Founded in 2020 by a team of former Wall Street professionals, we set out to create a platform that would democratize access to professional trading tools and education.

Today, we're proud to serve over 50,000 traders worldwide, helping them develop the skills and confidence needed to succeed in the financial markets.
